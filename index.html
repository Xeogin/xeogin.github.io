But why though?

To give a more consistent damage output for all aim skill levels as well as bringing more viability to auto/semi-auto firearms.

Other notable changes:

  • SLAMs only give 1 ammo per pick-up rather than 3. Initial reserve ammo for all guns, with the exception of the 357, has been increased by 1 clip and the SMG receiving 2 clips.

  • The AR2's orb aim assist has been nerfed; the search radius has been halved and guidance reduced by about a third.

  • Superchargers can still charge you to 200 armor, but they only have 150 stored, so you'll need to collect 50 batteries first if you want the max.

  • Prop mass and passthrough properties are standardized across all maps, making smaller one's less of a tripping hazard and giving a more consistent experience.

  • For speeds under 450, there is now a short window to assist with bhop timing to help folks using the space bar or with higher ping/latency.

  • Gravity (such as for killbox) & air acceleration (such as for surf) are changed per map to provide the intended experience of the creator.

  • The jump and surf maps have checkpoints (!cp) that are stored per player per map as well as !bring & !goto commands.

  • Robust anti-cheat and fixes in place, if you believe you've triggered a false positive or a bug, please report it on the hl2dm.community Discord.
